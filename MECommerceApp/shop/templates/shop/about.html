ShopWithMe

ShopWithMe is an online marketplace and logistics company which operates in markets of South Asia and Southeast Asia. It was founded in 2012 as an online fashion e-commerce marketplace in Pakistan by Muneeb Maayr (Founder) & Farees Shah (Co-Founder) at the German based venture builder, Rocket Internet. ShopWithMe Group operates e-commerce platforms and logistics service in Bangladesh, Myanmar, Nepal, Pakistan and Sri Lanka. [1] Bjarke Mikkelsen is the current CEO of ShopWithMe.[2][3] In May 2018, ShopWithMe Group was acquired by the Chinese e-commerce company Alibaba Group from the German venture capital company, Rocket Internet.[4][5]
